Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 6, 2026

1. Introduction

NorthApps Studio respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our mobile applications.

By using our applications, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree, please do not use our applications.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

We collect only the information that you voluntarily provide to us, such as:

  • App preferences and settings
  • Feedback, inquiries, or support messages sent by you

We do not require account registration and do not collect unnecessary or sensitive personal information.

Automatically Collected Information

When you use our applications, basic technical information may be collected automatically only if analytics or advertising services are integrated into the app, including:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • Application usage statistics
  • Crash reports and performance data
  • If analytics or advertising services are not integrated, no such data is collected
  • This information is used solely to improve app functionality, performance, and stability

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used only to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve our applications
  • Analyze app performance and usage patterns
  • Identify and fix technical issues
  • Respond to user feedback or support requests
  • Comply with applicable laws and legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or misuse your personal data.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share personal data with third parties.

Data is shared only if analytics or advertising services are integrated into the application and only to the extent necessary to support app functionality, performance improvement, or analytics.

If no such services are integrated, no data is shared with any third party.

Data may also be disclosed if required to comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes.

5. Data Storage and Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Third-Party Services

Our applications may integrate trusted third-party services such as analytics or advertising providers. These third parties operate under their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their data handling practices.

We encourage users to review the privacy policies of any third-party services used.

7. Age Restriction (18+)

Our applications are intended only for individuals who are 18 years of age or older.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that a minor under 18 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate action to remove such data.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information

You may exercise these rights by contacting us via email.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the "Last updated" date will be updated accordingly.
